Equipment

What is Equipment?

Equipment is any item that you can equip. Equipment gives bonuses to things like Attack, Strength, Defence and Health.

How do I get Equipment?

Equipment can be gained from various methods across the game, including Crafting, Enemy Drops, Events, or even secret rares found while training.
